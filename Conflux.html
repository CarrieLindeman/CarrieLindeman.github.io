Conflux
Design of a Streaming Aggregator

Co-Researcher & Co-Designer, with Shannon Fitzgerald, Ghazaleh Keshavarz, Shannon Cates • August 2019 - December 2019

Key Skills: Survey Design • User Interviews • Figma • HTML Mock-Up Design

Tasked with designing a web platform that aggregates all of a userâ€™s streaming platforms, my team created Conflux. This platform incorporates the best features from existing streaming platforms & television/movie sites, while introducing novel features to the experience.
Ours users centered on existing video content streamers who use web-based services. We were solving the issues that users have in using and juggling various streaming platforms. Our goal was to create such a seamless experience that users would default to Conflux to launch their streams.

This project happened in three phases:

Research

  • Survey distributed to >100 individuals
  • 1:1 interviews with 15 people
  • Creating empathy maps
  • Heuristic evaluation of existing streaming platforms

Design

  • Vision boarding for desired aesthetic
  • Creating style guide and color scheme
  • Iterating on logo design

Mock-Ups

  • Using Figma to demo initial interactions
  • Using HTML, CSS, and JSON to create an interactive mock-up
  • Connecting the mock-up with a database of film & TV

Key features include a comprehensive filtering menu, a feature to sync preferences with a partner/roommate/etc, and taking in-the-moment quizzes to get detailed suggestions based on your current mood.
