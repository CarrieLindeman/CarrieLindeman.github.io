Creating Video Content

Providing videos and digital content is beneficial for student learning and engagement. If you’re interested in creating a video or digital asset of your own schedule a time to chat with OTL’s Educational Technology (EdTech) team. EdTech is on a mission to support faculty development through the creation of digital teaching content and videos. They film and produce OTL’s TeachTalks interview series and provide creativity, support, and technical expertise to bring your instructional ideas to life.
