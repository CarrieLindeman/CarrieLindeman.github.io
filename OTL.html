Faculty Resource Website

For the Office of Transformational Learning

Researcher & Designer, with support from Nikk Bonneau, Moises Melano, Doireann Renzi • January 2020 - January 2022

Key Skills: Information Architecture • User Interviews • Figma • User Testing • Drupal Website Editing

Creating and distributing teaching materials to the faculty of the Smith School is a core function of the Office of Transformational Learning (OTL). This website was created to be an organized repository of resources and policies for faculty and staff to access in an "as-need" basis.

Research

  • 1:1 interviews with 12 faculty
  • Click-through user tests of site versions
  • Creating empathy maps
  • Needs analysis of faculty knowledge gaps

Design

  • Card sorts & information architecture activities
  • Research theming to make design decisions
  • Mock-up design in Illustrator & Figma

Test & Deploy

  • Releasing site Version 1 to collect usage data & testimonials
  • Iterating on design for Version 2
  • Deploying Version 2 to live users
  • Leading site Quality Assurance sessions with 12 team members

Process

We iterated on sketches and digital designs over the course of this project to develop the best product
